## Emergency Override Rules

1. **If something is on fire:** drop the schedule, fix it, log what happened.
2. **If energy is low on a Power day:** downgrade to Review. Don't waste a day forcing it.
3. **If you miss 2+ days:** don't try to "catch up." Reset priorities on the next {{REVIEW_DAY}}.
4. **Health > schedule.** Always.
